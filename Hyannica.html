2024 District Meet

by Hyannica Joy B. Calucin

KWLC's representative for badminton singles,
Averie Frances Calauad was announced as 1st runner up in the badminton
singles event in the recent Solano 2 District Sports Meet held in Brgy. San Juan,
Solano, Nueva Vizcaya. During the first set, Averie Frances scored 21 points while
the opposing player garnered 18 points. The first set was a struggle because Averie
was nervous but since her best Friends, teachers and coach were rooting for her she
was able to catch up. We all saw Averie's determination to win this for KWLC. On the
second set, there was still a little bit of nervousness but since she won the first set,
it gave her the confidence to win. Her score was 21 points and the opposing team had 11 points.
Although Averie only had a limited time to train, she was able to put in a good work for this year's
district meet. Your KWLC family is proud of you Averie Frances.
